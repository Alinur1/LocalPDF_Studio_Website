Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Your data belongs to you.

Our Privacy Commitment

LocalPDF Studio is built with privacy as our core principle. We believe that your data is yours, and we have designed our application to ensure complete data protection and user privacy.

What We Don't Do

  • No Data Collection

    We don't collect any of your data. Your privacy is our first priority. Everything stays on your device.

  • No Document Collection

    We don't collect any of your documents that you process using our software. Your files are completely yours.

  • No Device Information

    We don't collect any information that is related to your device or network. No hardware fingerprinting or IP tracking.

  • No Logging

    We don't collect any logs or crash reports. Your usage patterns remain private and unknown to us.

  • No Telemetry

    This application does not have any telemetry. We don't track what you do or how you use the software.

  • Works Offline

    This application does not require internet connection to work on your device. Full functionality without online access.

  • No Account Required

    This application does not require an account to use, thus you don't need to login. Start using immediately without registration.

Transparency Through Open Source

LocalPDF Studio is completely open-source. You can review the entire source code to verify our privacy claims. We have nothing to hide because we have nothing to hide from you.
